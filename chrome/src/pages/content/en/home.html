Dear ,

When you enroll, the student administration creates a personal file. This file is used and updated by the education administration and possibly other services of Hasselt University.

Through "My student file" you can, among other things:

  • View and update your personal information
  • (Re)enroll and check your enrollment details
  • Plan and monitor your study progress
  • View your exam results
  • Choose your bachelor's or master's thesis
  • Apply for a job as a student worker
  • And much more...

In the Spotlight

Learning Account

As of this academic year, the learning account is in effect. You can check your learning account via www.studentenportaal.be from the Flemish Ministry of Education and Training. Click here for an announcement from the Ministry of Education and Training.

For questions regarding the learning account, you can contact your faculty's educators.

Books/Course Material

Quickly collect your books and course materials for the course units in your program at the Campusshop Xod using your list of books/courses including the rack location of each book/course and the ground plan of the Campusshop in Diepenbeek and Hasselt. For books/course materials that are not in your program, please refer to the study guide.
